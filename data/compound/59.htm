Praseodymium Compounds
   Praseodymium(III) chloride
Praseodymium(III) chloride PrCl3
PrCl3 has been used to increase the activity of Pr6O11 catalysts, which can be used for the oxidation of methane to ethene. This process is becoming an important route to ethene for the manufacture of polyethylene (a common plastic)
