   Lutetium aluminum garnet (LuAG)
Lutetium aluminum garnet (LuAG) Al5Lu3O12
A ceramic material that has been proposed for use as a high refractive index lens material. When doped with cerium it also exhibits scintillation with visible luminescence, making it a candidate for high energy photon detection.
