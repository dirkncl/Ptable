Antimony pentoxide Sb2O5
It is used in a variety of industrial processes. It finds use as a flame retardant in ABS and other plastics, a flocculant in the production of titanium dioxide and is sometimes used in the production of glass, paint and adhesives.
Antimony trifluoride SbF3
: Highly Corrosive :
A light grey crystalline material often used in dyeing and pottery.
Antimony trioxide Sb2O3
: Highly Corrosive :
Antimony trioxide is the most important commercial compound of antimony. It is used as a flame retardant for textiles and polymers, an opacifying agent for glasses, ceramics and enamels and as a pigment.
