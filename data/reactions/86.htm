Reactions of Radon
Reactions with water
Radon does not react with water.
Reactions with air
Radon gas does not react with air.
Reactions with halogens
Radon only reacts with fluorine to form radon(II) fluoride, RnF6 although the exact details of this are unknown.
Reactions with acids
Radon gas does not react with acids.
Reactions with bases
Radon gas does not react with acids.
