Isotopes of Astatine
Notes
Astatine has no stable isotopes.
Astatine has 41 known isotopes, all of which are radioactive; the longest-lived isotope is 210At which has a half-llife of 8.1 hours. The shortest-lived isotope is 213At which has a half-llife of 125 nanoseconds.
