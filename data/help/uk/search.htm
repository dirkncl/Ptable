Searching
There are two different ways to search;
By keyword, across all the PT pages.
By element property(ies).
 
Type in your search request;
Searching for one word will just search for occurences of that word across all of the documents (except index pages).
When searching for more than one word you can display the results in one of two ways;
1.
Pages that contain ALL of your search terms will be labelled with , pages that match at least one search term will be labelled with .
2.
Only pages that contain all of your search terms will be displayed, and marked with a .
- - - -
Use the button to expand all of the search nodes, use the button to collapse all of the search nodes.
Your search results will be categorised on which Periodc Table documents they appear in;
Elements
Glossary
Biography
Isotopes
Compounds
Documents
Reactions
Production / Occurrence
 
You can search for elements based on one of ten properties.
Melting Point
Boiling Point
Atomic Radius
Atomic Weight
Density
Enthalpy of Fusion
Enthalpy of Vaporisation
Heat Capacity
Thermal Conductivity
Thermal Expansion
You can combine upto six properties together to narrow the search.
Select the property and choose either elements that are ">=" (greater than or equal to), "=" (equal to) or "<=" (less than or equal to) the value entered in the "Value" box.
