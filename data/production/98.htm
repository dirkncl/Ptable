Occurrence and Production of Californium
Synthesis
Was first created by bombarding a microgram-sized target of 242Cm with 35 MeV alpha particles (helium nuclei) in the 60-inch Berkeley cyclotron which produced atoms of 245Cf (half-life 44 minutes) and a single free neutron.
