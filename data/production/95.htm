Occurrence and Production of Americium
Synthesis
Americium is produced by bombarding plutonium-239 with high energy neutrons. This formed plutonium-240, which was itself bombarded with neutrons. The plutonium-240 changed into plutonium-241, which then decayed into americium-241 through beta decay.
