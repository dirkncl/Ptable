Zambia
Beryllium
In 2006, Zambia produced approx. 7 tonnes of beryl (Al2Be3Si6O18), one of the most important commercial compounds of beryllium. World production of beryl in 2006 was around 500 tonnes.
Cobalt
In 2006, Zambia's mine production of cobalt was 4556 tonnes. Total world production of cobalt from mining, in 2006, was 59000 tonnes.
Copper
In 2006, Zambia's production of copper by mining was 502000 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
In 2006, Zambia produced 497200 tonnes of refined copper, total production of refined copper was 17.2 million tonnes.
Selenium
In 2006, Zambia produced approx. 10 tonnes of selenium.
Gold
In 2006, Zambia's mine production of gold was 180 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
