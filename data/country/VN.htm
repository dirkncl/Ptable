Vietnam
Calcium
In 2006, Vietnam produced approx. 3000 tonnes of fluorspar (CaF2). World production was around 5.5 million tonnes.
Vietnam's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was 5000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Titanium
In 2006, Vietnam produced approx. 360000 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 500 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
Chromium
In 2006, Vietnam produced approx. 90000 tonnes of chromium ores and concentrates. Total worldwide production in 2006 was 19.2 million tonnes.
Iron
Vietnam's production of iron ore in 2006 was approx. 1.5 million tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Copper
In 2006, Vietnams' production of copper by mining was 11400 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
In 2006, Vietnams produced 4800 tonnes of refined copper, total production of refined copper was 17.2 million tonnes.
Zirconium
In 2006, Vietnam produced 27000 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4), the main commercial source of zirconium.
Tin
Vietnam's mine production of tin in 2006 was approx. 3500 tonnes. World production in 2006 was 321 thousand tonnes.
Vietnam's smelter production of tin in 2006 was 2500 tonnes. World smelter production in 2006 was 340 thousand tonnes.
Barium
In 2006, Vietnam produced 90000 tonnes of barytes (BaSO4), the main source of barium. World production of barytes in 2006 was 8.8 million tonnes.
Gold
In 2006, Vietnam's mine production of gold was approx. 3 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2006, Vietnam produced approx. 3500 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2006 was 3.5 million tonnes.
