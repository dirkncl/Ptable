Sweden
Iron
Sweden's production of iron ore in 2006 was 23.302 million tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Production of pig iron in Sweden in 2006 was 3.701 million tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.
Copper
In 2006, Sweden's production of copper by mining was 86746 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
In 2006, Sweden produced 229241 tonnes of refined copper, total production of refined copper was 17.2 million tonnes.
Zinc
In 2006, Sweden produced 210000 tonnes of zinc, around 2% of the world total.
Selenium
In 2006, Sweden was the world's third largest producer of selenium, approx. 120 tonnes.
Gold
In 2006, Sweden's mine production of gold was 6.848 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
