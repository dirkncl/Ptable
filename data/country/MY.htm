Malaysia
Titanium
In 2006, it produced 45649 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 16921 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
Iron
Malaysia's production of iron ore in 2006 was 667082 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Production of pig iron in Malaysia in 2006 was 1.5 million tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.
Zirconium
In 2006, Malaysia produced 1690 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4), the main commercial source of zirconium.
Tin
Malaysia's mine production of tin in 2006 was 2398 tonnes. World production in 2006 was 321 thousand tonnes.
Malaysia's smelter production of tin in 2006 was approx. 23000 tonnes. World smelter production in 2006 was 340 thousand tonnes.
Barium
In 2006, Malaysia produced 910 tonnes of barytes (BaSO4), the main source of barium. World production of barytes in 2006 was 8.8 million tonnes.
Rare Earths
It is estimated that in 2006, Malaysia produced 200 tons of rare earth oxides.
Malaysia is estimated to have produced 700 tons of monazite in 2006. Monazite is an important ore for thorium, lanthanum, and cerium.
Gold
In 2006, Malaysia's mine production of gold was 3.497 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2006, Malaysia's production of refined lead was 55000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2006 was 8 million tonnes.
Thorium
It's believed that Malaysia has reserves of around 4.5 tonnes of monazite, the primary thorium mineral.
