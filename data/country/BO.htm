Bolivia
Lithium
The most important deposit of lithium, with around 50% of the world's reserves, are to be found in the Salar de Uyuni area of Bolivia.
Arsenic
In 2006, Bolivia produced 120 tonnes of white arsenic (As2O3).
Tin
Bolivia's mine production of tin in 2006 was 17669 tonnes. World production in 2006 was 321 thousand tonnes.
Bolivia's smelter production of tin in 2006 was approx. 13500 tonnes. World smelter production in 2006 was 340 thousand tonnes.
Antimony
In 2005 Bolivia was the third biggest producer of antimony, with around 3.5% (5226 tonnes) of the world total.
Barium
In 2006, Bolivia produced 8943 tonnes of barytes (BaSO4), the main source of barium. World production of barytes in 2006 was 8.8 million tonnes.
Tungsten
In 2006, Bolivia produced 870 tonnes of tungsten. Total world production of tungsten in 2006 was 74400 tonnes.
Gold
In 2006, Bolivia's mine production of gold was 9.628 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2006, Bolivia produced 11955 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2006 was 3.5 million tonnes.
Bismuth
In 2006, Bolivia produced 155 tonnes of bismuth, total world production of bismuth in 2006 was around 5200 tonnes.
