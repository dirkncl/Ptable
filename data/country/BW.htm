Botswana
Carbon (Diamond)
Botswana contains commercially viable diamond deposits. It is estimated that Botswana has reserves of around 225 million carats.
Cobalt
In 2006, Botswana's mine production of cobalt was 303 tonnes. Total world production of cobalt from mining, in 2006, was 59000 tonnes.
Nickel
Botswana's mine production of nickel in 2006 was 26762 tonnes. Worldwide mine production of nickel in 2006 was 1.526 million tonnes.
Copper
In 2006, Botswana's production of copper by mining was 24255 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
Gold
In 2006, Botswana's mine production of gold was 3.02 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
