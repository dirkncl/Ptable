Namibia
Carbon (Diamond)
Namibia produced 1.45 million carats of diamonds in 2003.
Calcium
In 2006, Namibia produced 132249 tonnes of fluorspar (CaF2). World production was around 5.5 million tonnes.
Copper
In 2006, Namibia's production of copper by mining was 6262 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
Zinc
In 2006, Namibia produced around 425000 tonnes of zinc, around 1.7% of the world total.
Gold
In 2006, Namibia's mine production of gold was 2.79 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2006, Namibia produced 11830 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2006 was 3.5 million tonnes.
Uranium
Namibia was the sixth largest producer of uranium in 2006, 2782 tonnes, around 7% of the world total.
