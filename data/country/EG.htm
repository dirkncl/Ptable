Egypt
Calcium
In 2006, Eqypt produced approx. 7700 tonnes of fluorspar (CaF2). World production was around 5.5 million tonnes.
Eqypt's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 120 thousand tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Titanium
In 2006, Egypt produced approx. 125000 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), one of only two commercially important titanium ores.
Iron
Eqypt's production of iron ore in 2006 was approx. 2.5 million tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Production of pig iron in Eqypt in 2006 was 4.2 million tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.
Copper
In 2006, Egypt produced approx. 18000 tonnes of refined copper, total production of refined copper was 17.2 million tonnes.
