Nigeria
Iron
Nigeria's production of iron ore in 2006 was approx. 8000 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Tin
Nigeria's mine production of tin in 2006 was 1500 tonnes. World production in 2006 was 321 thousand tonnes.
Barium
In 2006, Nigeria produced approx. 30000 tonnes of barytes (BaSO4), the main source of barium. World production of barytes in 2006 was 8.8 million tonnes.
Lead
In 2006, Nigeria's production of refined lead was approx. 5000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2006 was 8 million tonnes.
