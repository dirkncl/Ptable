Argentina
Lithium
Argentina is currently the second largest producer of lithium in the world, where it is recovered from brine pools.
Argentina has deposits of spodumene (the most important of the lithium ores) LiAlSi2O6.
Calcium
In 2006, Argentina produced 8278 tonnes of fluorspar (CaF2). World production was around 5.5 million tonnes.
Argentina's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 1.2 million tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Iron
Production of pig iron in Argentina in 2006 was 4.426 million tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.
Copper
In 2006, Argentina's production of copper by mining was 180144 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
In 2006, Argentina produced approx. 16000 tonnes of refined copper, total production of refined copper was 17.2 million tonnes.
Strontium
In 2006, Argentina produced approx. 19822 tonnes of strontium minerals.
Barium
In 2006, Argentina produced 6276 tonnes of barytes (BaSO4), the main source of barium. World production of barytes in 2006 was 8.8 million tonnes.
Gold
In 2006, Argentina's mine production of gold was 44.131 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2006, Argentina produced 12778 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2006 was 3.5 million tonnes.
In 2006, Argentina's production of refined lead was 49064 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2006 was 8 million tonnes.
