Jordan
Calcium
Jordan's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was 333710 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Bromine
In 2006, Jordan produced 95.5 tonnes, world wide production was 481 thousand tonnes.
Jordan and Israel recover bromine from the bromine-rich brine wells from the Dead Sea waters, where bromine is found at up to 50000ppm.
